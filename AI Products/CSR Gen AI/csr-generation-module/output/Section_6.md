# 6. Investigators and Study Administrative Structure

The study was sponsored by BioNTech SE and conducted by Pfizer, who also served as a collaborator.

The administrative structure of the study included Principal Investigators at each of the 169 study sites, an independent Data Monitoring Committee (DMC), and for Phase 1, an Internal Review Committee (IRC). The DMC, composed of external members, was responsible for the ongoing monitoring of participant safety. The IRC was responsible for reviewing safety data to permit dose escalations and make recommendations on study progression during Phase 1. Statistical analyses were performed by an unblinded statistical team at Pfizer. Three blinded case reviewers, who were medically qualified Pfizer staff members, were responsible for reviewing all potential COVID-19 illness events.

The Principal Investigators were responsible for conducting the study at their respective sites in compliance with the protocol and Good Clinical Practice (GCP) guidelines. They were also responsible for the well-being of the study participants at their site.
