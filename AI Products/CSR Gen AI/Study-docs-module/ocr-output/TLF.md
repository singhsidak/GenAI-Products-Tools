# Section 9: Investigational Plan (Pages 37–54)

9.1. Overall Study Design and Plan This is a Phase 1/2/3, randomized, multinational, placebo-controlled, observer-blind, dose finding, vaccine candidate–selection, and efficacy study in healthy individuals. The study evaluated the safety, tolerability, and immunogenicity of 3 different SARS-CoV-2 RNA vaccine candidates against COVID-19 and the Phase 2/3 efficacy of 1 selected candidate (BNT162b2).
